How to Play

Classic Dominoes follows standard match-end rules:

  • Match the numbers on your tiles to the open ends of the chain on the board.
  • Double tiles are placed sideways.
  • If you can't play, draw from the boneyard. If the boneyard is empty, pass your turn.
  • The first player to empty their hand wins the round and scores the points remaining in the opponent's hand.
  • If both players are blocked, the player with fewer points in hand wins.

Choosing an End

When a tile can be played on both the left and right ends of the chain, tap the tile to select it, then choose which end to play on using the buttons that appear.

Frequently Asked Questions

The AI seems too fast/slow

The AI takes a randomized 1.5–3 seconds to "think" before playing, simulating a natural pace. It always plays its highest-value playable tile.

My stats disappeared

Stats are stored locally on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, stats will be reset. Premium purchases will be restored, but stats cannot be recovered.
