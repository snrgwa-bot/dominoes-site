### Description

```
Challenge yourself in the timeless game of dominoes! Play against a smart computer opponent in this beautifully crafted iOS experience.

FEATURES:
• Classic match-end dominoes with standard rules
• Smart AI opponent that draws, plays strategically, and keeps every game exciting
• Smooth spring-powered animations and polished transitions
• Multiple table and tile themes to personalize your experience
• Track your wins, losses, and scores across games
• Double tiles go sideways, chains snake across the table
• Draw from the boneyard when you can't play
• One-time Premium purchase to remove ads and unlock all themes

HOW TO PLAY:
Tap a playable tile to place it on the board. Match the numbers on your tiles to the open ends of the chain. If a tile fits both ends, choose which side to play. Can't play? Draw from the boneyard. Empty your hand first to win!

Simple to learn, endlessly replayable. The perfect game for a quick break or a long session.
```

## How to Archive & Upload from Xcode

1. Open the project in Xcode
2. Select **Product → Destination → Any iOS Device**
3. Select **Product → Archive**
4. When archive completes, the **Organizer** window opens
5. Select the archive → click **Distribute App**
6. Choose **App Store Connect** → **Upload**
7. Keep all checkboxes default → click **Upload**
8. Wait for processing (5-15 minutes) in App Store Connect
