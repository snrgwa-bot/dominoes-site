Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Dumitru Bumbu ("we", "us", or "our") built Classic Dominoes ("the App") as a free app supported by ads, with an optional premium upgrade. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, or any other personal data to use the App.

All game data (scores, statistics, preferences) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Advertising

The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device Identifier — Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for ad targeting.
  • Coarse Location — Approximate location based on IP address for regional ad relevance.
  • Usage Data — Information about how you interact with ads (impressions, clicks).
  • Advertising Data — Information about advertisements displayed to you.

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time Premium purchase to remove ads and unlock all themes. All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains third-party advertising which may collect device identifiers as described above.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Game data stored locally on your device persists until you delete the App.

Your Choices

  • Ad Tracking — You can limit ad tracking in your device settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Remove Ads — Purchase Premium to remove all third-party advertising and associated data collection.
  • Delete Data — Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored game data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
